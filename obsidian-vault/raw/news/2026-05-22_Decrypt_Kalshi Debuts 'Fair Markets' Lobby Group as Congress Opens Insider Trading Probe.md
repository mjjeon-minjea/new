## [[Kalshi]] Debuts 'Fair Markets' Lobby Group as Congress Opens Insider Trading Probe

**[[Decrypt]]** reports that [[Kalshi]] has launched a new lobbying group called the **'Fair Markets' lobby group** in response to the ongoing investigation by Congress into insider trading. The group aims to advocate for policies that promote fair and transparent markets, particularly for cryptocurrencies.

The news comes as Congress is actively pursuing legislation to address the issue of insider trading in the cryptocurrency market. [[Kalshi]] believes its lobbying efforts will help shape regulations that benefit both investors and the industry as a whole.

**Here's a breakdown of the key points:**

* **[[Kalshi]] Launches Lobby Group:** The company has formed a dedicated lobby group to advocate for policies promoting fair markets, particularly in the cryptocurrency space. This move follows Congress's investigation into insider trading in cryptocurrencies.
* **Focus on Fair Markets:** [[Kalshi]] emphasizes that their focus is on creating fairer and more transparent markets for all participants, especially those involved in cryptocurrencies. They believe this will benefit both investors and the industry as a whole.
* **Impact on Regulations:** The lobbying efforts aim to influence regulations regarding insider trading in the cryptocurrency market. This could lead to new rules and policies that address the issue effectively.
